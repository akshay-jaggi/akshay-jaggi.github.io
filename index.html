Hi everyone!

I'm a graduate student at Tsinghua University, studying as a Schwarzman Scholar. I'm currently applying for MD/PhD programs with the intention of completing a PhD in Computational Biology.

Previously, I majored in Biology and Computer Science at Duke, conducted research on precision tuning of GPCR pathways at the Rajagopal Lab, and studied applications of machine learning in health with the Duke Crucible. Outside of academics, I served as an EMT for Duke EMS, taught hundreds of CPR courses throughout Durham, interned as a SAV Fellow, and ran a healthtech startup.

If you want to talk about GPCRs or machine learning in health, please reach out!
