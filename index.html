Hi everyone!

I'm an incoming graduate student at Tsinghua University, where I'll be studying as a Schwarzman Scholar. I'm interested in the intersection of medicine and computing.

Previously, I majored in Biology and Computer Science at Duke University. While at Duke, I worked at the Rajagopal Lab to study cell signaling and how certain "smart" drugs can eliminate common drug side-effects. I also did health artificial intelligence research, served as an Emergency Medical Technician for Duke EMS, and ran a (now) defunct healthtech startup. In my free time, I've visited 81 restaurants in "the foodie capital of the South" - see recommendations here.

If you want to talk about G Protein-Coupled Receptors or machine learning in health, please reach out!
