Hi everyone!

I'm an incoming graduate student at Tsinghua University, where I'll be studying as a Schwarzman Scholar. I'm interested in the intersection of medicine and computing.

Previously, I majored in Biology and Computer Science at Duke University. While at Duke, I worked at the Rajagopal lab to study cell signaling and how certain "smart" drugs can eliminate common drug side-effects. I also did health AI research, served as an EMT for Duke EMS, and ran a (now) defunct healthtech startup.
